Kalpataru Grove

An integrated ecosystem of digital Sanskrit projects.

  • HANSEL is a home where new Sanskrit texts live
  • Skrutable helps with common text processing tasks, including OCR
  • SETI provides an overview of available textual resources
  • Pāṇḍitya visually presents networks of Sanskrit authors, their works, and e-texts online
  • Vātāyana reveals intertextual relationships inside and across texts
Finally, creator Tyler serves as curator, helping the grove to flourish and benefit visitors.

Anyone can view Kalpataru Grove web-app traffic and status with Firewatch (u: kalpataru, p: grove).
