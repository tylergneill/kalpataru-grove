Kalpataru Grove

An integrated project ecosystem for enriching Sanskrit text data online.

  • HANSEL is a home where new Sanskrit texts live.
  • Skrutable helps with common text processing tasks, including OCR.
  • SETI grounds online e-text collections to Pandit Project IDs.
  • Pāṇḍitya visualizes networks of authors, works, and online e-texts.
  • Vātāyana reveals intertextual relations within and across texts.
